Our Story

A place to slow down and savor.

"An was so nice and she gave us free pastries too. Will be back for sure — this is one of my favorite coffee shops now." — Google Review

Tucked between Bellevue's towers, Café An'Clair is a place that feels genuinely different. Calm, green, full of shade — perfect for a sunny morning or a quiet afternoon.

We believe good coffee is about care: in the sourcing, in the craft, and in the welcome you receive when you walk through our doors.
